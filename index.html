How each moves damage is determined:

Shoot: Uses Three Pointers Made and Three Point Percentage [If Three Point Percentage is above or equal 40% = bonus damage]

Defend: Uses Steals and Blocks [If Steals or Blocks are above or equal to 2 = bonus damage]

Pass: Uses Assists [If Assists are above or equal to 7 = bonus damage]

Box-out: Uses Rebounds [If Rebounds are above or equal to 7 = bonus damage]
